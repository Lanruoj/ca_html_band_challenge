Band members

Archy Marshall, being the head of the band, writes most of the music. Over the years, however, his band has grown to include a full host of musicians for his live shows.

  • Archy Marshall (voice, guitar, keyboards)
  • James Wilson (bass, vocals)
  • George Bass (drums)
  • Jack Towell (guitar)
  • Jamie Isaac (electronics)
  • Ignacio Salvadores (saxophone)
