About the band

King Krule is an alias used by 27 year old UK musician Archy Marshall. He has been releasing music since 2010, under various aliases, until he formed King Krule in 2011, releasing the "King Krule" EP in November that year.

The band is notable for blending genres, with heavy influences from jazz, punk, folk, hip-hop, and various others.
